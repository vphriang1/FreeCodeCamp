Master Jiraiya

The Evolution Of Our Beloved Sensei

Genin Years

Lord Jiraiya was a Leaf Shinobi like no other. This handsome sizzling devil of the leaf started off as a rascal trouble maker. His mischief included spying on girls, pulling pranks, and compulsive boasting.

He also got in touch with his spirit animal. Through the guidance of toads, Lord Jiraiya learned how to utilize the rare technique of sage mode in toad form.

Chunin Years

Jonin Years

Despite his smug background, he grew up to become an extraordinary shinobi. A master of disguise and esponiage, Lord Jiraiya was entrusted to dozens of A level missions. He traveled the land taking on many challenges many dared not to do.

There is so much to say about this legendary hero. Needless to say, his greatness is so superb, we can not honor him enough.

Lineage

Lord Jiraiya was trained by the third Hokage and then became the teacher of the fourth Hokage. When the third Shinobi war commenced, Lord Jiraiya triumphed as one of the three legendary Sanin. For those who do not know what a Sanin is, a legendary Sanin is a war hero whom put an end to a great war.

We of the Konoha Village Newspaper is proud to present this section of our paper to arguably one of Konoha's finest legends: The gallant, toad Sage, the hero of the day, Master Jiraiya.

Profession

As a leaf village Shinobi, Lord Jiraiya undoubtly brought much peace and prosperity to the village. However he was much more.
